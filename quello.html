Quello - Traveling Safe During a Pandemic

UX/UI Case Study

Overview

During the peak of the pandemic, people still seemed to be traveling frequently across the U.S. Most likely, there are many people who have health concerns and want to able to feel safe during travel.

See the lead up to Quello's Hi-Fi app design through research practices and analysis

The Problem

People feel hesitant to travel currently. How can we make them feel good about travel during the pandemic?

The Solution

Quello will assist users with real time Covid safety data on their destination as well as budget tracking.
